# Perplexity Pro Research Prompt: Meta Ads Image Best Practices

Copy and paste this prompt into Perplexity Pro for deep research on ad image creation.

---

## Research Prompt

```
Act as a Meta Ads creative strategist specializing in the Mexican wellness and personal development market. Research the following topics for 2025-2026:

**1. Meta Ads Image Performance Data**
- What image styles perform best for lead generation ads in wellness/coaching niche?
- Optimal text-to-image ratio for Facebook/Instagram feed ads
- Color psychology that resonates with Mexican audiences 30-55 years old
- Photo vs illustration vs abstract imagery conversion rates
- Which performs better: professional photography or authentic "raw" style?

**2. Mexican Market Visual Preferences**
- Cultural visual cues that build trust (warmth, authenticity, family values)
- Imagery that converts for high-ticket wellness offerings ($2,000+ retreats)
- What NOT to use: visual patterns that underperform in Mexico
- How does Mexican wellness imagery differ from US/European markets?

**3. Avatar-Specific Creative Guidelines**
For each avatar, what visual elements resonate best:
- **Emprendedores (30-45)**: What imagery speaks to successful but unfulfilled entrepreneurs? What settings, poses, expressions work?
- **Wellness Seekers (28-50)**: What imagery attracts people already interested in meditation/mindfulness? Nature, indoor, people vs objects?
- **C-Suite Executives (40-60)**: What premium visual codes work for senior leaders? Forbes-style or more relaxed?

**4. Technical Specifications for 2026**
- Optimal dimensions for Advantage+ placements
- Text overlay best practices (position, font, contrast)
- Scroll-stopping techniques for feed ads
- Safe zones for different placements (Feed, Stories, Reels)
- Image quality requirements and compression tips

**5. AI-Generated Images in Ads**
- Current Meta policies on AI-generated ad images (2025-2026)
- Best practices for AI image prompts in wellness niche
- How to avoid "uncanny valley" issues with AI portraits
- Disclosure requirements for AI imagery in Mexico

**6. Competitor Analysis**
- What imagery do successful Mexican wellness brands use?
  - Insights Timer Mexico
  - Mindvalley Mexico
  - Local retreat centers (Tepoztlán, Tulum)
  - Mexican life coaches with successful ad campaigns
- Ad creative trends in the "éxito vs vacío" (success vs emptiness) messaging space

**7. Creative Testing Framework**
- A/B testing best practices for image variations
- How many creative variants to test per ad set
- When to refresh creatives to avoid fatigue

Provide actionable recommendations with specific examples. Include data/statistics where available.
```
