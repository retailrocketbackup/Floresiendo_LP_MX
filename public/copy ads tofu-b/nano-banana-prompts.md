# Nano Banana Pro Image Prompts: TOFU-B Campaign

6 prompts for generating new ad images. Copy each prompt individually into Nano Banana Pro.

---

## Style Guide (Match Existing Assets)

- **Aesthetic**: Organic & Warm
- **Color Palette**: Earth tones, golden hour lighting, warm whites, greens, coral accents
- **Mood**: Contemplative, transformative, aspirational but grounded
- **Quality**: Photorealistic, magazine-quality
- **Format**: 1080x1080px square (also request 1080x1920 for Stories)

---

## Prompt 1: M1 - Entrepreneur Pausa Estratégica

**For Ad**: M1 (Emprendedores × Ruido Mental)
**Headline**: "¿Tu mente nunca para?"

```
Photorealistic image of a Mexican entrepreneur (man, 35-40 years old) in a modern home office, pausing from work. Eyes closed, hands resting on closed MacBook laptop. Natural window light creating soft shadows. Neutral earth-tone clothing (linen shirt). Expression of peaceful pause. Clean minimal desk with plant. Style: editorial photography, shallow depth of field. Warm color grading. No text. 1080x1080 square format.
```

**Post-processing**: Add text overlay "PAUSA ESTRATÉGICA" in elegant sans-serif font

## Quality Checklist

Before selecting final images, verify:
- [ ] No AI artifacts (extra fingers, distorted features)
- [ ] Faces look natural and authentic
- [ ] Lighting is warm and consistent with existing assets
- [ ] Subject is clearly Mexican/Latino
- [ ] Expression matches the ad's emotional angle
- [ ] Resolution is at least 1080x1080
