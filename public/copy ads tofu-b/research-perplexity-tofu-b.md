## SECTION 5: RISK MITIGATION \& COMMON PITFALLS

### No-Show Prevention: The Biggest ROI Lever

Your biggest risk is the 40-60% no-show rate for free in-person events. This alone can make the event logistically impossible (empty venue, wasted catering budget, diminished attendee experience). **Action items:**

1. **Implement the full WhatsApp reminder sequence** (detailed above). This is non-negotiable for free events.
2. **Consider a refundable \$25 deposit** for Conferencia Vida Perfecta. Even a "deposit" (money held, returned after attendance) drops no-show from 50% to 5%. The friction of putting down a card is psychologically equivalent to a paid event.[^22][^23]
3. **Ask for commitment**: In the 24-hour WhatsApp reminder, include "Reply YES to confirm" and only send the venue address/link to responders. This secondary commitment signal drops no-shows by 15-20%.[^18]

### Lead Quality vs. Quantity Tradeoff

Your pain point dropdown field adds friction (-5.8% conversion) but increases lead quality (+28% Sales Acceptance Rate). For Conferencia Vida Perfecta, this is worth it because you're trying to attract qualified leads for a \$2,000+ retreat. For Meditaci√≥n Gratis, skip it entirely. Keep virtual meditation signups ultra-simple.

### Budget Allocation Discipline

Resist the temptation to increase budget on underperforming ad sets in Week 1-2. The learning phase requires patience. Premature scaling wastes money. Wait 500+ conversions per ad set before deciding to scale or kill.

### Competitive Audience Bleed

Running two simultaneous campaigns to overlapping audiences (e.g., both targeting "Meditation" + "Personal Growth") creates auction competition. Meta's algorithm will bid against itself, raising costs. Solution: Use **Advantage+ targeting with clear, distinct messaging** so Meta learns the difference (Meditaci√≥n = virtual calm; Conferencia = burnout recovery). If CPL rises above \$3, consider splitting geographic targeting (Meditaci√≥n nationally, Conferencia CDMX-focused).
